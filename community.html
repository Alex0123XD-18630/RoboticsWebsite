Building Bridges

Our Community

At LASA Robotics, we believe that it's not enough for just our members to enjoy STEM. We want everyone to get the chance.

The Shine Partnership

We are proud to partner with Trinity Episcopal School's Shine program to support families from under-resourced communities in Austin.

Our students volunteer to combat summer learning loss by providing dynamic STEM curriculum and robotics demonstrations at no cost to families. This partnership allows us to reach students who might not otherwise have access to robotics education.

Pillars of Our Community

Mentorship

We connect high schoolers with industry professionals for guidance, while simultaneously having our high schoolers mentor elementary and middle school students.

Volunteering

Service is at the heart of Purple Haze. Our team logs over 500 hours of community service annually, extending our impact far beyond the competition field.

Diversity & Inclusion

We strive to create a culture rooted in innovation and inclusivity, ensuring that every student feels empowered to lead and collaborate with confidence.
