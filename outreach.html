Outreach & Impact

We believe STEM education should be accessible to everyone. Through our camps, software libraries, and community events, we strive to spread the message of FIRST beyond our school walls.

Summer Program

SMART Camps

Since 2006, Purple Haze has hosted SMART (Science, Mathematics, and Robotics Technology) camps every summer. These camps introduce students (Grades 2-8) to the world of STEM through hands-on learning with LEGO Spike Prime and VEX V5 kits.

Our varsity students act as counselors, designing the curriculum and mentoring the next generation of engineers.

Open Source Resources

PurpleLib

We believe in open source. PurpleLib is a Java library we developed to help other FRC teams control Swerve drivetrains, manage logging, and handle hardware abstraction. It is free for any team to use and modify.

View on GitHub

FIRST PHinder

Finding a local robotics team shouldn't be hard. We built "FIRST PHinder," an interactive dashboard and map that helps families in Austin find FLL and FTC robotics teams near them, filtering by grade level and zip code.
