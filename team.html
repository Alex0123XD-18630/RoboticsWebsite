Our History & Future

Our Teams

From our flagship FRC team to our FTC development programs, LASA Robotics has a rich history of engineering excellence since 2000.

Varsity / FRC Est. 2000

Team 418: Purple Haze

Purple Haze is one of the oldest and most decorated high school robotics teams in Central Texas. Competing in the FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC), we simulate a real-world engineering environment.

Our team is divided into specialized sub-teams: Mechanical Design (CAD), Fabrication, Electrical, Programming, and Business/Marketing. Every student finds their niche, contributing to building a 120lb industrial robot in just 6 weeks.

25+ Years Active
World Championships

FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC)

Our FTC teams serve as a development ground where newer members learn the fundamentals of Java programming, iterative design, and mechanical assembly on a smaller, more agile scale.

FTC Team

4290: Bot-tleship

A foundational team that focused on rapid prototyping and agile design. 4290 served as a training ground for future FRC leaders, specializing in compact, high-efficiency robot designs. Known for their "never give up" attitude in matches.

#AgileDesign #Prototyping
FTC Team

5998: Such A Pain

Known for their creative problem solving and unique chassis designs. Team 5998 emphasized the "Engineering Notebook" and documentation, often winning the Think Award for their detailed technical process and journey throughout the season.

#Documentation #ThinkAward
